Hobbies

Outside of my academic life, I enjoy staying active and engaging with my community.

Cricket

I play cricket for a local club in the Seattle-Redmond area. It's more than just a sport for me; it's a way to connect with the community and stay disciplined. We practice regularly and compete in local tournaments, which keeps my competitive spirit alive.

Ultimate Frisbee

I played Ultimate Frisbee for my university club. The fast-paced nature of the game and the emphasis on "Spirit of the Game" taught me valuable lessons in teamwork and sportsmanship. I still enjoy tossing the disc around whenever I get the chance.

Hiking

Living in the Pacific Northwest, I've fallen in love with hiking. I have explored many trails around Seattle, from the lush forests to the rugged mountain peaks. It's my favorite way to disconnect and recharge.
